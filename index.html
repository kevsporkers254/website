About Us

Nairobi's Most Trusted Pork Limited

Located at Ridgeways, Kiambu Road, we've been serving the community with the finest pork cuts since 2018. Our commitment to quality, hygiene, and freshness has made us the go-to limited company for pork lovers across Kenya.

We source our pork from certified local farms in Kiambu and Nakuru, ensuring every cut meets the highest health and safety standards. Whether you're preparing nyama choma, stew, or a special family recipe, we've got the perfect cut for you.
